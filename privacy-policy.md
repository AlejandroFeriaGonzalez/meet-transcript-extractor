# Privacy Policy — Meet Transcript Extractor

**Last updated: April 27, 2026**

Meet Transcript Extractor does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

## What the extension does
It reads the visible transcript text from the active Google Drive tab
and saves it as a .txt file directly to your device.

## Data handling
- No data is sent to external servers
- No analytics or tracking of any kind
- No user accounts or authentication required
- All processing happens locally in your browser

## Permissions
Permissions are used solely to read transcript content from the active
Drive tab and save the resulting file to your device.
